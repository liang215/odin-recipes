Burger

Description

I love smash cheeseburgers, and this recipe is just like they are cooked on a flat top in a diner. The perfect amount of crust on the patties is what makes these smash cheeseburgers my fave!.

OlThis is the first recipe ever for burgers cooked indoors that my guys have devoured and then asked when I'm making them again! I did 2 patties per person with shredded cheddar between them, bacon, ketchup and served them on griddled buttered pretzel rolls. A++

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun
  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Heat up bun
  2. Cook meat
  3. Put Tomato slices on meat
  4. Put lettuce on meat
  5. Spread Mayonnaise on bun
